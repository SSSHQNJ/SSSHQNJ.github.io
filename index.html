About Us

Since 1986, Systematic Software Solutions, Inc. (SSS) has served the retail industry, providing software development and integration services, as well as offering its best-in-class trickle-polling software.

Products

Our ISRS "trickle-polling" software allows retailers to keep their finger on the "pulse" of their selling operations, moving POS sales floor data to headquarters, allowing real-time visibility and decision-making. Our STS/1 intelligent file transfer product ensures that the most up-to-date information (pricing, customer lists, tax rates, etc.) are delivered to the systems that depend on it. Both products are fully automated and boast exceptionally high availability statistics.

Services

In addition to our licensed products, SSS designs and develops custom solutions for many aspects of our customers' businesses. Equally at home in the mainframe and open system spaces, SSS has implemented solutions that extend and enhance legacy systems. In addition, we have architected and implemented systems and processes that allowed our customers to migrate to modern platforms with a minimum of distruption and cost.

Customers

SSS has partnered with a wide range of companies in the retail and communications industries, including: Walt Disney World, Sears, Macy's, Saks Fifth Avenue, Hudson's Bay Company, Rose's and Bell Labratories.
